Our Origins

The Cocoa Craft Philosophy

NTR Honey Ocean Chocolates and Cookies was founded on a simple, yet revolutionary premise: that indulgence should be pure, natural, and absolute. Inspired by the rich, complex flavors of single-origin cocoa beans, our chocolatiers set out to create a line of confections that showcase the depth of real dark chocolate.

We believe that mass-produced chocolates mask the intricate, natural notes of cocoa. By roasting premium beans in-house and manually tempering our chocolate, we unlock a completely unique, velvety flavor profile that lingers beautifully on the palate.

Located in the heart of Bangalore, we pride ourselves on being a boutique confectioner. Each batch is individually formulated, manually tempered, and artfully decorated to ensure that our brand remains synonymous with absolute luxury.

Ingredients

Crafted with Purity

Gourmet Cocoa Butter

Pure, cold-pressed cocoa butter extracted from premium cocoa beans. It provides our chocolates with a signature glossy shine, a clean snap, and a velvety melt-in-the-mouth texture.

Single-Origin Cocoa

We source our premium cacao beans directly from organic estates. From fruity Madagascar pods to deep, earthy South American beans, we roast in-house for rich aroma profiles.

Pure Creamery Butter

We use rich, slow-churned sweet cream butter for our cookie batters. This ensures a delicate, melt-in-the-mouth crumb structure and a comforting, golden-baked finish.

Timeline

Our Milestone Journey

2023

The Creative Conception

Chocolatier trials began in Bangalore to source single-origin estates, perfecting roasting profiles and recipes for our dark chocolate truffles.

2024

Boutique Launch

We launched our first commercial micro-kitchen in Bangalore, offering local door-to-door delivery. Introduced our flagship sea-salt dark chocolate cookies.

2025

Bespoke Corporate Gifting

Expanded our kitchen capacities and introduced custom luxury chocolate assortments and hampers for weddings, corporate celebrations, and festive hampers.

2026

Digital Expansion

Launched online ordering and WhatsApp order routing, bringing fresh luxury chocolates and custom cookies directly to tables across all of Bangalore.
